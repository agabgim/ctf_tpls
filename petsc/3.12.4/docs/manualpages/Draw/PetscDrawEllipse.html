PetscDrawEllipse

 Draws an ellipse onto a drawable.

Synopsis

#include "petscdraw.h" 
PetscErrorCode  PetscDrawEllipse(PetscDraw draw, PetscReal x, PetscReal y, PetscReal a, PetscReal b, int c)
Not collective

Input Parameters

draw - The drawing context
x,y - The center
a,b - The major and minor axes lengths
c - The color
