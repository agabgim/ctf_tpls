DMSwarmSetCellDM

 Attachs a DM to a DMSwarm

Synopsis

#include "petscdmswarm.h"   
PETSC_EXTERN PetscErrorCode DMSwarmSetCellDM(DM dm,DM dmcell)
Collective on dm

Input parameters

dm - a DMSwarm
dmcell - the DM to attach to the DMSwarm

Notes

The attached DM (dmcell) will be queried for point location and neighbor MPI-rank information if DMSwarmMigrate() is called.

See Also

DMSwarmSetType(), DMSwarmGetCellDM(), DMSwarmMigrate()

beginner
