DMPlexTSGetGeometryFVM

Input Parameter

dm -The DM

Output Parameters

facegeom - The values precomputed from face geometry
cellgeom - The values precomputed from cell geometry
minRadius - The minimum radius over the mesh of an inscribed sphere in a cell
