PetscDrawSave

 Saves a drawn image

Synopsis

#include "petscdraw.h" 
PetscErrorCode  PetscDrawSave(PetscDraw draw)
Not Collective

Input Parameters

draw -the drawing context

Notes: this is not normally called by the user, it is called by PetscDrawClear_X() to save a sequence of images.
